My Hobbies

Talking about my hobbies. Well, I always like everything that related to science and technology. I was 5 years old, when I play my very first game on my daddy's phone. The first game that I tried was Platypus. It's a 2D game, the graphic looks like it mades from clay and you play as an "Earth's Guardian", shooting at the Alien's aircrafts.

Aaaand, finally I got my first laptop at 7th grade. It was the best laptop ever back then (at least for me lol). The specs it's not too great, but it's quite comfortable for me, as long as I can do my assignments and of course, light gaming. I installed some of the famous games at that time and I was seeking for more comfortable gaming experience.
